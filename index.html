The guide

Meet Aldacir

Aldacir has spent years learning South Florida's wild places — and the national parks beyond them — camera and binoculars in hand.

That same attention to detail goes into every property he looks after around Boca Raton. Book a morning in the field or a hand with your home — either way you get someone who shows up prepared and genuinely cares how it turns out.

  • 10+ years in the field
  • 2 languages spoken
  • 1:1 private & small groups

What I do

Two kinds of expertise, one careful pair of hands

In the field

Eco-tours & birdwatching

Half- and full-day trips into the Everglades, wetlands and back-country few visitors ever reach. We go when the light and the birds are best — early morning and the hour before dusk.

In the field

Photography expeditions

Outings built around your camera. Get close to owls, raptors and wading birds with someone who knows their habits — and the patience it takes. All skill levels welcome.

At home

Handyman & property care

Off the trail, the same care goes into your home. Repairs, maintenance and custom builds for houses and vacation properties across Boca Raton — done right, on time.
